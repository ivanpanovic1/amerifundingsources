Delinquent Debt portfolios

Advantages of Selling Delinquent Accounts

  • MARKETABLE ASSET - Legally, a portfolio of non-performing accounts receivable is a marketable asset. It is a great opportunity for companies to turn a non-cash-flowing commodity into a cash flowing commodity.
  • SELLING vs. SUB-CONTRACTING - Sub-contracting the collections with an external agency causes the owner to be doubly exposed from a liability perspective. The growing trend for many portfolio owners is first to collect in-house, and then to liquidate through a sale.
  • ELIMINATION OF NON-PERFORMING ACCUMULATION - Our two step plan reduces or eliminates tracking on old accounts placed with agencies. First, all old accounts are purchased in a bulk sale; and second, a flow-forward contract can be put into place.
  • CASH VALUE UP FRONT - Any institution owning receivable pools would rather have $100 up front than 12 payments of $10 spread out over a year.
  • PAYMENT ON EVERY ACCOUNT - It is extremely rare that a collection agency will collect money on every single account. We will pay cash on every legally collectible account
  • INDEMNIFICATION OF SELLER - The buyer will sign an indemnification clause in the purchase agreement, which will protect the seller from activities of the buyer which may cause legal action.
