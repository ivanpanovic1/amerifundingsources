About us

Amerifunding Sources is a group of investors that specialized in the structure and purchase of Notes secure by Real Estate, and the purchase of other future payments (cash-flows), offering a lump sum of cash to individuals who hold assets in the form of structured settlements and annuities. we also purchase life insurance policies from consumers.

Over the years, the company has built an unparalleled infrastructure that combines dynamic sales and marketing techniques, a strong credit and underwriting culture, proprietary technology and a highly skilled and enthusiastic workforce. With several trillion dollars in total assets backing annuities, structured settlements and life insurance policies in the United States, we see a tremendous opportunity to help Americans who need cash now. Our company is your best source for professional, value driven low cost service.

Established in 2005 and online since 2005, our Firm have decades of collective and comprehensive knowledge and experience. Meaning that we are able to provide the most value and innovation per your dollar spent. We provide a satisfaction guarantee for all services provided.
